This is a Sudoku app, made with SvelteKit. It offers several features:

- thousands of Sudoku puzzles to solve
- fill in candidates
- highlights of peers and numbers
- undo every step
- create your own Sudoku puzzles
- shareable URLs
- game state is saved in localStorage
